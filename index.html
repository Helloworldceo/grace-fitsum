About Grace / ስለ ግሬስ

The Art of Meaningful Celebration

Grace Fitsum is a creative visionary dedicated to the art of celebration. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for storytelling, she transforms personal dreams into breathtaking realities. Grace believes that every event should be as unique as the people it honors, blending sophisticated modern aesthetics with the heartfelt traditions that matter most. Her approach is calm, professional, and deeply personalized, ensuring that every client feels like a guest at their own masterpiece.
